Information on Team 7

Kakashi Hatake

Kakashi Hatake is one of the most powerful and world famous shinobi.He is mostly known as Kakashi of Sharingan.He is the Sixth Hokage of The Hidden Leaf.

Uzumaki Naruto

Uzumaki Naruto is unpredictable knuckledhead ninja.He carries the legacy of 4th Hokage Minato Namikaze. He possess the Chakra of Nine Tail Fox.

Uchiha Sasuke

Uchiha Sasuke is the last member of Uchiha Clan. He becomes a rouge ninja.He is brother of Itachi Uchiha. He possess the ultimate power of Amaterasu which was gifted by Itachi.

Haruno Sakura

Haruno Sakura is the student of Fifth Hokage. She is the best medical ninja of The Hidden Leaf.
